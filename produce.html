Overview

PROduce is a responsive web application designed to help users find recipes suited to their daily lives and are quick and easy to make.

The Process

Problems

As time goes on, apps and websites dedicated to helping people prep and cook food have become extremely widespread.Frequent problems that users face include:

  1. Meals taking too long to cook
  2. Not having enough choice for those with dietary requirements eg. vegan, lactose intolerant etc.
  3. Text and instructions too hard to follow

Solutions

To improve the overall process for users, the app needs to contain solutions to the common problems:

  1. Information next to the recipe title containing cooking time and serving amounts
  2. Filter option for those who have dietary restrictions
  3. Clear step by step instructions for users to follow

JTBD and Feature requirements

I was able to better understand the users goals by using the Jobs To Be Done method. I narrowed down the types of functions they wished were available based on wants and needs. I then chose the most necessary features to implement in my app.

  1. A bookmark or save function to keep track of recipes and a library where they can access the ones they've saved.
  2. A meal planner for families who decide in advance what they're going to cook based on calendar date.
  3. The option to add ingredients from a recipe to a shopping list. This stops users from forgetting items they need when shopping. This feature will include a conversion switch to help with measurements.
